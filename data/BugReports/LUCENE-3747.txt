               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Now that Unicode 6.1.0 has been released, Lucene/Solr should support it.</p> 
                  <p>JFlex trunk now supports Unicode 6.1.0.</p> 
                  <p>Tasks include:</p> 
                  <ul> 
                   <li>Upgrade ICU4J to v49 (after it's released, on 2012-03-21, according to <a href="http://icu-project.org" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://icu-project.org</a>).</li> 
                   <li>Use <tt>icu</tt> module tools to regenerate the supplementary character additions to JFlex grammars.</li>
