                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>This property is not documented in the Tuning Derby guide, but it is referred to <br> in the developer's guide in a couple of places with dangling pointers to the Tuning Guide:</p> 
                  <p><a href="http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevdvlp27715.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevdvlp27715.html</a><br> <a href="http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevcsecure60146.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevcsecure60146.html</a><br> <a href="http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevadvjdbc41138.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://db.apache.org/derby/docs/dev/devguide/cdevadvjdbc41138.html</a></p> 
                  <p>Googling, I find it documented in older Cloudscape docs e.g. in<br> <a href="http://www.novell.com/documentation/extendas37/docs/help/java/jdkee/cloudscape/doc/html/coredocs/proper42.htm#1014327" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://www.novell.com/documentation/extendas37/docs/help/java/jdkee/cloudscape/doc/html/coredocs/proper42.htm#1014327</a>,</p> 
                  <p>but not in Cloudscape 10 docs, e.g. <a href="http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/cldscp10/topic/com.ibm.cloudscape.doc/perf63.htm" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/infocenter/cldscp10/topic/com.ibm.cloudscape.doc/perf63.htm</a></p> 
                  <p>which could indicate it was removed for some reason. So, either we<br> should document it anew or remove the references to it. If we decide to keep it, we should also consider<br> derby.database.noAutoBoot (not currently documented).</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
