               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The implicit properties (specifically solr.core.name) work fine for Solr startup, but on core RELOAD, they no longer exist, so configurations that use them result in the core not initializing.</p> 
                  <p>Problem discovered on 4.5.0 RC4, works fine in 4.4.0.</p>
