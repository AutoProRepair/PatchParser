                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Database can be assumed to be same as in Derby - 1205 Jira issue</p> 
                  <p>SELECT PORT1.PORT_ID FROM T_RES_PORT PORT1, T_VIEW_ENTITY2PORT ENTITY2PORT WHERE ENTITY2PORT.PORT_ID = PORT1.PORT_ID</p> 
                  <p>This works fine in 10.1.2.1 but fails thereafter complaining that Comparison between INTEGER and CHAR is not supported</p> 
                  <p>for some reason, it thinks one of the PORT_ID columns is a character, when in reality both are integers.</p> 
                  <p> SELECT DISTINCT <br> ZONE.ZONE_ID ZONE_ID, <br> PORT2ZONE.ZONE_MEMBER_ID <br> FROM <br> T_RES_ZONE ZONE left outer join T_VIEW_PORT2ZONE PORT2ZONE on <br> ZONE.ZONE_ID = PORT2ZONE.ZONE_ID , T_RES_FABRIC FABRIC </p> 
                  <p>In this query, it is complaining that one of the columns is a VARCHAR and cannot be compared to INTEGER, when clearly this is not the case...</p> 
                  <p>Same issue</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
