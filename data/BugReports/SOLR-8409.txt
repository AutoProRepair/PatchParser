                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When providing an expression like </p> 
                  <div class="code panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="codeContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre class="code-java">stream=search(people, fl=<span class="code-quote">"id,first"</span>, sort=<span class="code-quote">"first asc"</span>, q=<span class="code-quote">"presentTitles:\"</span>chief executive officer\<span class="code-quote">" AND age:[36 TO *]"</span>)
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>the following error is seen.</p> 
                  <div class="code panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="codeContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre class="code-java">no field name specified in query and no <span class="code-keyword">default</span> specified via <span class="code-quote">'df'</span> param
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>I believe the issue is related to the \" (escaped quotes) and the spaces in the q field. If I remove the spaces then the query returns results as expected (though I've yet to validate if those results are accurate).</p> 
                  <p>This requires some investigation to get down to the root cause. I would like to fix it before Solr 6 is cut.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
