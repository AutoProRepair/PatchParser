                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>It looks like something changed that now returns all fields requested from lucene, not just the ones request from solr.</p> 
                  <p>This is the difference between 'fields' and 'okFieldNames' in SolrReturnFields.</p> 
                  <p>The logic here:<br> <a href="https://github.com/apache/lucene-solr/blob/master/solr/core/src/java/org/apache/solr/search/SolrReturnFields.java#L141" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">https://github.com/apache/lucene-solr/blob/master/solr/core/src/java/org/apache/solr/search/SolrReturnFields.java#L141</a></p> 
                  <p>adds all the 'fields' to 'okFieldName'</p> 
                  <p>I think that should be removed</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
