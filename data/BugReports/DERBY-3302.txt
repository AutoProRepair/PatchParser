               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When logical undo is performed on a database with territory-based collation, you may get a NullPointerException in SQLChar.getCollationKey() because SQLChar.getLocaleFinder() returns null.</p> 
                  <p>This bug was reported on derby-user: <a href="http://thread.gmane.org/gmane.comp.apache.db.derby.user/8253" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://thread.gmane.org/gmane.comp.apache.db.derby.user/8253</a></p>
