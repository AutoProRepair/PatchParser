                  <p>schema.xml<br> &lt;?xml version="1.0" ?&gt;<br> &lt;schema name="${solr.core.name}" version="1.5" xmlns:xi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XInclude"&gt;<br> &lt;fieldType name="int" class="solr.TrieIntField" precisionStep="0" positionIncrementGap="0"/&gt;<br> &lt;fieldType name="long" class="solr.TrieLongField" precisionStep="0" positionIncrementGap="0"/&gt;<br> &lt;fieldType name="date" class="solr.TrieDateField" precisionStep="0" positionIncrementGap="0"/&gt;<br> &lt;field name="<em>version</em>" type="long" indexed="true" stored="true"/&gt;<br> &lt;field name="id" type="int" indexed="true" stored="true" multiValued="false" /&gt;<br> &lt;field name="date" type="date" indexed="true" stored="true" multiValued="false" /&gt;<br> &lt;uniqueKey&gt;id&lt;/uniqueKey&gt;<br> &lt;defaultSearchField&gt;id&lt;/defaultSearchField&gt;<br> &lt;/schema&gt;</p>

                  <p>Insert into testshard2:<br> &lt;add&gt;<br> &lt;doc&gt;<br> &lt;field name="id"&gt;2&lt;/field&gt;<br> &lt;field name="date"&gt;2013-06-20T12:51:00Z&lt;/field&gt;<br> &lt;/doc&gt;<br> &lt;/add&gt;</p>
