                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When running</p> 
                  <p> s.executeUpdate("create table t (i integer)");<br> PreparedStatement p = c.prepareStatement("insert into t values<img class="emoticon" src="/jira/images/icons/emoticons/help_16.png" height="16" width="16" align="absmiddle" alt="" border="0">");<br> for (int i=0; i&lt;N; i++) </p> { p.setInt(1,i); p.addBatch(); } 
                  <p> System.out.println("Ok");<br> p.executeBatch();</p> 
                  <p>If N is 9000<br> The server reports:</p> 
                  <p>524272<br> java.lang.ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException: 524272<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DDMWriter.startDdm(DDMWriter.java:315)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.writeSQLCARD(DRDAConnThread.java:4937)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.writeSQLCARDs(DRDAConnThread.java:4898)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.writeSQLCARDs(DRDAConnThread.java:4888)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.checkWarning(DRDAConnThread.java:7239)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.parseEXCSQLSTT(DRDAConnThread.java:3605)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.processCommands(DRDAConnThread.java:859)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.drda.DRDAConnThread.run(DRDAConnThread.java:214)<br> agentThread<span class="error">[DRDAConnThread_3,5,main]</span></p> 
                  <p>While the client hangs in executeBatch().</p> 
                  <p>If N is 8000, the client gets the following Exception:<br> Exception in thread "main" org.apache.derby.client.am.BatchUpdateException: Non-atomic batch failure.
