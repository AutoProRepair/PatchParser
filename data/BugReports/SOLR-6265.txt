                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>As of r1612418, both the 4x and trunk svn trees seem to have a bug where any core specific init errors that occur on startup don't show up in the log until/unless someone attempts to access that core via HTTP.</p> 
                  <p>i'm not sure when exactly this bug was introduced, but it definitely isn't in 4.9.</p> 
                  <p>The impact on users, particularly new users, is that starting up solr with a mistake in your configs appears to work fine until you actually try to use solr and then you get ugly errors.</p> 
                 </div>
