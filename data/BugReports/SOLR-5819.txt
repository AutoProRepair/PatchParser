                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>As noted on the solr-user mailing list in response to the ANNOUNCE about the 4.7 ref guide, the size of the 4.4, 4.5 &amp; 4.6 PDF files were all under 5MB, but the 4.7 PDF was 30MB.</p> 
                  <p>We've determine that the root cause is a bug in confluence 5.0 (related to duplicating images) that is fixed in 5.4.3 – the next version Infra currently plans to upgrade to.</p> 
                  <p>Until such time as the upgrade is finished, a work arround is to use a manual pdf shrinking tool such as this one to eliminate the duplication...</p>
