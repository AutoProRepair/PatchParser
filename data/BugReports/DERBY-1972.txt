                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The Working With Derby book uses DITA tags in ways that are not always consistent with the usage in other books and that lead to some problems in the processed output. Some examples are the use of the &lt;varname&gt; tag in inappropriate places and the use of formatting tags within &lt;codeblock&gt; tags.</p> 
                  <p>There are also a few minor punctuation and syntax issues worth fixing.</p>
