                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I'd like to introduce the two new classes DataInput and DataOutput<br> that contain all methods from IndexInput and IndexOutput that actually<br> decode or encode data, such as readByte()/writeByte(),<br> readVInt()/writeVInt().</p> 
                  <p>Methods like getFilePointer(), seek(), close(), etc., which are not<br> related to data encoding, but to files as input/output source stay in<br> IndexInput/IndexOutput.</p> 
                  <p>This patch also changes ByteSliceReader/ByteSliceWriter to extend<br> DataInput/DataOutput. Previously ByteSliceReader implemented the<br> methods that stay in IndexInput by throwing RuntimeExceptions.</p> 
                  <p>See also <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-2125" title="Ability to store and retrieve attributes in the inverted index" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-2125">LUCENE-2125</a>.</p> 
                  <p>All tests pass.</p>
