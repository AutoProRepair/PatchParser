                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>If the ResultSet is so big that the scrollable resultset needs to back the hashtable to disk, and the resultset is not fully populated before a commit(),<br> a call to next() will fail with:</p> 
                  <p>ak136785@khepri35:/&lt;3&gt;db-derby-10.1.2.1-bin/lib&gt; java -cp /home/ak136785/devel/derbytesting/derbytest/build/classes/:./derby.jar derbytest.HoldabilityIssue<br> ERROR XSCB8: The btree conglomerate 1,141,037,436,752 is closed.<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.error.StandardException.newException(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.store.access.btree.BTreeController.insert(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.store.access.btree.index.B2IController.insert(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.store.access.DiskHashtable.put(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.store.access.BackingStoreHashtable.spillToDisk(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.store.access.BackingStoreHashtable.add_row_to_hash_table(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.store.access.BackingStoreHashtable.put(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.execute.ScrollInsensitiveResultSet.addRowToHashTable(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.execute.ScrollInsensitiveResultSet.getNextRowFromSource(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.execute.ScrollInsensitiveResultSet.getNextRowCore(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.execute.BasicNoPutResultSetImpl.getNextRow(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.jdbc.EmbedResultSet.movePosition(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.jdbc.EmbedResultSet.next(Unknown Source)<br> at derbytest.HoldabilityIssue.runTest1(HoldabilityIssue.java:72)<br> at derbytest.HoldabilityIssue.main(HoldabilityIssue.java:91)<br> ak136785@khepri35:/&lt;3&gt;db-derby-10.1.2.1-bin/lib&gt;</p> 
                  <p>The BtreeController is always initialized as non-holdable, and if this is changed, Derby has the following assert in closeForEndTransaction(..):<br> SanityManager.THROWASSERT("There is currently no requirement for a held btree conglomerate controller.");</p> 
                  <p>A simple test program is attached which reproduces this failure. It fails in the trunk and on the 10.1.2.1 release</p> 
                 </div>
