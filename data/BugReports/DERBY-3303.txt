                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Derby throws ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException when I try to execute SQL query shown below.</p> 
                  <p>This is a regression, since Derby 10.2.2.0 executes this query without problems.</p>
