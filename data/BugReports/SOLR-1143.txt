                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>If any shard is down in a distributed search, a ConnectException it thrown.</p> 
                  <p>Here's a little patch that change this behaviour: if we can't connect to a shard (ConnectException), we get partial results from the active shards. As for TimeOut parameter (<a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/SOLR-502" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/SOLR-502</a>), we set the parameter "partialResults" at true.</p> 
                  <p>This patch also adresses a problem expressed in the mailing list about a year ago (<a href="http://www.nabble.com/partialResults,-distributed-search---SOLR-502-td19002610.html" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://www.nabble.com/partialResults,-distributed-search---SOLR-502-td19002610.html</a>)</p> 
                  <p>We have a use case that needs this behaviour and we would like to know your thougths about such a behaviour? Should it be the default behaviour for distributed search?</p> 
                 </div>
