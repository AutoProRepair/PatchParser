                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I am trying to upgrade our production solr instance from 4.7 to 5.3.1. Unfortunately when I do load testing I find the MatchAllDocsQuery is much slower in solr 5.3.1 compare to 4.7. (solr 5.3.1 is faster in load test with queries other than MatchAllDocsQuery). I asked solr-user and discussed with Yonik Seeley. He confirmed that he can see the problem too comparing solr 5.3.1 and 4.10.</p> 
                  <p>here is the query I use:</p> 
                  <div class="code panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="codeContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre class="code-java">q={!cache=<span class="code-keyword">false</span>}*:*&amp;fq=+categoryIdsPath:1001&amp;fl=id&amp;start=0&amp;rows=2&amp;debug=<span class="code-keyword">true</span>
</pre> 
                   </div>
                  </div> 
                  <p>for me the query is consistently about 60-70% slower on solr5 than solr4.</p> 
                  <p>Yonik mentioned in his email "For me, 5.3.1<br> is about 5x slower than 4.10 for this particular query."</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
