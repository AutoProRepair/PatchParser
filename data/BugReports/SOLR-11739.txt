                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Solr is supposed to reject duplicated async IDs, however, if the repeated IDs are sent fast enough, a race condition in Solr will let the repeated IDs through. The duplicated task is ran and and then silently fails to report as completed because the same async ID is already in the completed map. </p> 
                 </div>
