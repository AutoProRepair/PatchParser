                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Attached</p> 
                  <ul class="alternate" type="square"> 
                   <li>PDF with instructions on how to replicate.</li> 
                   <li>data.xml to replicate index</li> 
                  </ul> 
                  <p>The f.&lt;field&gt;.facet.mincount option on a distributed search gives inconsistent list of buckets on a range facet.</p> 
                  <p>Experiencing that some buckets are ignored when using the option "f.&lt;field&gt;.facet.mincount=1".</p> 
                  <p>The Solr logs do not indicate any error or warning during execution.<br> The debug=true option and increasing the log levels to the FacetComponent do not provide any hints to the behaviour.</p> 
                  <p>Replicated the issue on both Solr 4.5.1 &amp; 4.8.1.</p> 
                  <p>Example, </p> 
                  <p>Removing the f.&lt;field&gt;.facet.mincount=1 option gives the expected list of buckets for the 6 documents matched.</p> 
                  <p> &lt;lst name="facet_ranges"&gt;<br> &lt;lst name="price"&gt;<br> &lt;lst name="counts"&gt;<br> &lt;int name="0.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="50.0"&gt;1&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="100.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="150.0"&gt;3&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="200.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="250.0"&gt;1&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="300.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="350.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="400.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="450.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="500.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="550.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="600.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="650.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="700.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="750.0"&gt;1&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="800.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="850.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="900.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="950.0"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;/lst&gt;<br> &lt;float name="gap"&gt;50.0&lt;/float&gt;<br> &lt;float name="start"&gt;0.0&lt;/float&gt;<br> &lt;float name="end"&gt;1000.0&lt;/float&gt;<br> &lt;int name="before"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="after"&gt;0&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;int name="between"&gt;2&lt;/int&gt;<br> &lt;/lst&gt;<br> &lt;/lst&gt;</p>
