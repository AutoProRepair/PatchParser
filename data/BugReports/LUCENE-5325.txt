               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Spinoff from <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-5298" title="Allow aggregating facets by any ValueSource" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-5298"><del>LUCENE-5298</del></a>: ValueSource and FunctionValues are abstract APIs which exist under the queries/ module. That causes any module which wants to depend on these APIs (but not necessarily on any of their actual implementations!), to depend on the queries/ module. If we move these APIs under core/, we can eliminate these dependencies and add some mock impls for testing purposes.</p> 
                  <p>Quoting Robert from <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-5298" title="Allow aggregating facets by any ValueSource" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-5298"><del>LUCENE-5298</del></a>:</p> 
                  <blockquote> 
                   <p>we should eliminate the suggest/ dependencies on expressions and queries, the expressions/ on queries, the grouping/ dependency on queries, the spatial/ dependency on queries, its a mess.</p>
                  </blockquote> 
                  <p>To add to that list, facet/ should not depend on queries too.</p>
