               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I'm looking at the DataImport code as of Solr v1.3 and using it with Postgres and very large data sets and there some improvement suggestions I have.</p> 
                  <p>1. call setReadOnly(true) on the connection. DIH doesn't change the data so this is obvious.</p> 
                  <p>2. call setAutoCommit(false) on the connection. (this is needed by Postgres to ensure that the fetchSize hint actually works)</p> 
                  <p>3. call setMaxRows(X) on the statement which is to be used when the dataimport.jsp debugger is only grabbing X rows. fetchSize is just a hint and alone it isn't sufficient.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
