                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Users have reported databases that will not boot with stack traces showing:<br> Caused by: java.lang.ArrayIndexOutOfBoundsException<br> at java.lang.System.arraycopy(Native Method)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.store.raw.data.AllocPage.ReadContainerInfo(Unknown Source)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.store.raw.data.FileContainer.readHeader(Unknown Source)</p> 
                  <p>I suggest the code be changed to catch the out of bounds and turn it<br> into a StandardException and include as much runtime information as<br> possible so that the underlying problem can be diagnosed. Information<br> should include sizes of both arrays, the amount of data being copied ("N"),<br> and possibly a hex dump of the source array.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
