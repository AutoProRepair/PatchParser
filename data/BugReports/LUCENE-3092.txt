                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I created this simply Directory impl, whose goal is reduce IO<br> contention in a frequent reopen NRT use case.</p> 
                  <p>The idea is, when reopening quickly, but not indexing that much<br> content, you wind up with many small files created with time, that can<br> possibly stress the IO system eg if merges, searching are also<br> fighting for IO.</p> 
                  <p>So, NRTCachingDirectory puts these newly created files into a RAMDir,<br> and only when they are merged into a too-large segment, does it then<br> write-through to the real (delegate) directory.</p> 
                  <p>This lets you spend some RAM to reduce I0.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
