                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>

               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The DIH example (solr/example/example-DIH/solr/rss/conf/rss-data-config.xml) is broken again. See associated issues.</p> 
                  <p>Below is a config that should work.</p> 
                  <p>This is caused by Slashdot seemingly oscillating between RDF/RSS and pure RSS. Perhaps we should depend upon something more static, rather than an external service that is free to change as it desires.</p> 
                  <div class="code panel" style="border-width: 1px;">
                   <div class="codeContent panelContent"> 
                    <pre class="code-xml"><span class="code-tag">&lt;dataConfig&gt;</span>
    <span class="code-tag">&lt;dataSource type=<span class="code-quote">"URLDataSource"</span> /&gt;</span>
    <span class="code-tag">&lt;document&gt;</span>
        &lt;entity name=<span class="code-quote">"slashdot"</span>
                pk=<span class="code-quote">"link"</span>
                url=<span class="code-quote">"http://rss.slashdot.org/Slashdot/slashdot"</span>
                processor=<span class="code-quote">"XPathEntityProcessor"</span>
                forEach=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item"</span>
                transformer=<span class="code-quote">"DateFormatTransformer"</span>&gt;
				
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"source"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/channel/title"</span> commonField=<span class="code-quote">"true"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"source-link"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/channel/link"</span> commonField=<span class="code-quote">"true"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"subject"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/channel/subject"</span> commonField=<span class="code-quote">"true"</span> /&gt;</span>
			
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"title"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/title"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"link"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/link"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"description"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/description"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"creator"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/creator"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"item-subject"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/subject"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"date"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/date"</span> dateTimeFormat=<span class="code-quote">"yyyy-MM-dd<span class="code-quote">'T'</span>HH:mm:ss"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"slash-department"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/department"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"slash-section"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/section"</span> /&gt;</span>
            <span class="code-tag">&lt;field column=<span class="code-quote">"slash-comments"</span> xpath=<span class="code-quote">"/RDF/item/comments"</span> /&gt;</span>
        <span class="code-tag">&lt;/entity&gt;</span>
    <span class="code-tag">&lt;/document&gt;</span>
