                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>HTML line breaks (&lt;br&gt;, &lt;BR&gt;, &lt;br/&gt;, ...) seem to be removed during extraction of content from HTML-Files. They need to be replaced with a empty space.</p> 
                  <p>Test-File:<br> &lt;html&gt;<br> &lt;head&gt;<br> &lt;title&gt;Test mit HTML-Zeilenschaltungen&lt;/title&gt;<br> &lt;/head&gt;<br> &lt;p&gt;<br> word1&lt;br&gt;word2&lt;br/&gt;<br> Some other words, a special name like linz&lt;br&gt;and another special name - vienna<br> &lt;/p&gt;<br> &lt;/html&gt;</p> 
                  <p>The Solr-content-attribute contains the following text:<br> Test mit HTML-Zeilenschaltungen <br> word1word2<br> Some other words, a special name like linzand another special name - vienna</p> 
                  <p>So we are not able to find the word "linz".</p> 
                  <p>We use the ExtractingRequestHandler to put content into Solr. (wiki.apache.org/solr/ExtractingRequestHandler)</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
