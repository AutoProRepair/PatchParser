                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Today <tt>BytesRefArray</tt> uses one int (<tt>int[]</tt>, overallocated) per<br> value to hold the length, but for dimensional points these values are<br> always the same length. </p> 
                  <p>This can save another 4 bytes of heap per indexed dimensional point,<br> which is a big improvement (more points can fit in heap at once) for<br> 1D and 2D lat/lon points.</p>
