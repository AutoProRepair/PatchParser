                  <p>I've been playing with the idea of changing the Kuromoji viterbi<br> search to be 2 passes (intersect, backtrace) instead of 4 passes<br> (break into sentences, intersect, score, backtrace)... this is very<br> much a work in progress, so I'm just getting my current state up.<br> It's got tons of nocommits, doesn't properly handle the user dict nor<br> extended modes yet, etc.</p> 
                  <p>One thing I'm playing with is to add a double backtrace for the long<br> compound tokens, ie, instead of penalizing these tokens so that<br> shorter tokens are picked, leave the scores unchanged but on backtrace<br> take that penalty and use it as a threshold for a 2nd best<br> segmentation...</p>
