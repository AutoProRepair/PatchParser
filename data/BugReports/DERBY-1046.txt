                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The JVMInfo class is included twice in derbyclient.jar, as<br> org.apache.derby.iapi.services.info.JVMInfo and<br> org.apache.derby.shared.common.info.JVMInfo. The only one of them<br> actually used by the client code is the one found in<br> org.apache.derby.shared.common.info.</p> 
                  <p>org.apache.derby.iapi.services.info.JVMInfo is also included in<br> derby.jar, so one could run into problems if the classpath contains<br> derbyclient.jar and derby.jar with different versions.</p> 
                 </div>
