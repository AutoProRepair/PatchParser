               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The junit tests have been made to run with security manager.<br> Until now, using the org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.harness classes, there was exception logic that stopped the j9 jvms from running with security manager, but that's now changed for the junit tests.</p> 
                  <p>For instance, the test store/bootAllTest.junit fails with the following error:<br> There was 1 error:<br> 1) testSettingBootAllPropertyWithHomePropertySet(org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.store.BootAllTest)java.security.AccessControlException: Access denied (java.util.PropertyPermission framework read)<br> at java.security.AccessController.checkPermission(AccessController.java:74)<br> at java.lang.SecurityManager.checkPermission(SecurityManager.java:612)<br> at java.lang.SecurityManager.checkPropertyAccess(SecurityManager.java:366)<br> at java.lang.System.getProperty(System.java:319)<br> at java.lang.System.getProperty(System.java:301)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestUtil$1.run(TestUtil.java:177)<br> at java.security.AccessController.doPrivileged(AccessController.java:147)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestUtil.getFramework(TestUtil.java:174)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestUtil.getDataSourcePrefix(TestUtil.java:391)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestUtil.getSimpleDataSource(TestUtil.java:330)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestUtil.getDataSource(TestUtil.java:324)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.util.TestDataSourceFactory.getDataSource(TestDataSourceFactory.java:47)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.TestConfiguration.openConnection(TestConfiguration.java:296)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseJDBCTestCase.openConnection(BaseJDBCTestCase.java:197)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.functionTests.tests.store.BootAllTest.setUp(BootAllTest.java:58)<br> at org.apache.derbyTesting.junit.BaseTestCase.runBare(BaseTestCase.java:76)</p> 
                 </div>
