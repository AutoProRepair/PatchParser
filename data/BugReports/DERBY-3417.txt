                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I tried a stopSlave on the slave side of the replication system and<br> found the below</p> 
                  <p>ij&gt; connect 'jdbc:derby://localhost:1528/replicationdb;stopSlave=true';<br> ERROR XRE41: DERBY SQL error: SQLCODE: -1, SQLSTATE: XRE41, SQLERRMC: XRE41</p> 
                  <p><a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/DERBY-3205" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/DERBY-3205</a> says</p> 
                  <p>ERROR XRE41: Replication operation 'failover' or 'stopSlave' failed because the connection with the master is working. Issue the 'failover' or 'stopMaster' operation on the master database instead.</p> 
                  <p>needs to be printed.</p> 
                  <p>I am not sure if this is a generic case for client server replication messages.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
