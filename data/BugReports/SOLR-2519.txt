                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>Spinoff from: <a href="http://lucene.markmail.org/thread/ww6mhfi3rfpngmc5" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://lucene.markmail.org/thread/ww6mhfi3rfpngmc5</a></p> 
                  <p>The text fieldType in schema.xml is unusable for non-whitespace<br> languages, because it has the dangerous auto-phrase feature (of<br> Lucene's QP – see <a href="https://issues.apache.org/jira/browse/LUCENE-2458" title="queryparser makes all CJK queries phrase queries regardless of analyzer" class="issue-link" data-issue-key="LUCENE-2458"><del>LUCENE-2458</del></a>) enabled.</p> 
                  <p>Lucene leaves this off by default, as does ElasticSearch<br> (<a href="http://http://www.elasticsearch.org/" class="external-link" rel="nofollow">http://http://www.elasticsearch.org/</a>).</p> 
                  <p>Furthermore, the "text" fieldType uses WhitespaceTokenizer when<br> StandardTokenizer is a better cross-language default.</p> 
                  <p>Until we have language specific field types, I think we should fix<br> the "text" fieldType to work well for all languages, by:</p> 
                  <ul> 
                   <li>Switching from WhitespaceTokenizer to StandardTokenizer</li> 
                  </ul> 
                  <ul> 
                   <li>Turning off auto-phrase</li> 
                  </ul> 
                 </div> 
                </div> 
               </div>
              </div>
