                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>The parser that the <tt>ManagedSynonymFilterFactory</tt> uses to parse the JSON resource into a synonym map does not parse multi-term synonyms in the expected way.</p> 
                  <p>If the synonym </p> {"foo bar":"baz"} 
                  <p> is added to the managed resource, the expected behavior is that the multi-term synonym "foo bar" will be mapped to the synonym "baz".</p> 
                  <p>In the <tt>analyze</tt> method of <tt>SynonymMap.Parser</tt>, multiple origin terms are concatenated with a separating <tt>SynonymMap.WORD_SEPARATOR</tt>, but the <tt>analyze</tt> method is not used by the parser in the <tt>ManagedSynonymFilterFactory</tt>.</p> 
                  <p>As a workaround, multi-term synonyms can be uploaded separated by a null character, i.e., uploading </p> {"foo\u0000bar":"baz"} 
                  <p> works.</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
