                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>I modified BaseTokenStreamTestCase to assert that the start/end<br> offsets match for graph (posLen &gt; 1) tokens, and this caught a bug in<br> Kuromoji when the decompounding of a compound token has a punctuation<br> token that's dropped.</p> 
                  <p>In this case we should leave hole(s) so that the graph is intact, ie,<br> the graph should look the same as if the punctuation tokens were not<br> initially removed, but then a StopFilter had removed them.</p> 
                  <p>This also affects tokens that have no compound over them, ie we fail<br> to leave a hole today when we remove the punctuation tokens.</p> 
                  <p>I'm not sure this is serious enough to warrant fixing in 3.6 at the<br> last minute...</p> 
                 </div> 
                </div>
