                <h2 class="toggle-title">Description</h2>
               </div>
               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When running stress.multi on a machine with 32 hardware execution threads, I observed the following assert failure in two independent runs:</p> 
                  <hr> 
                  <p>2008-07-18 02:15:14.415 GMT Thread<span class="error">[Thread-8,5,workers]</span> (XID = 94699), (SESSIONID = 16923), (DATABASE = mydb), (DRDAID = null), Failed Statement is: insert into a values (1)<br> org.apache.derby.shared.common.sanity.AssertFailure: ASSERT FAILED<br> at org.apache.derby.shared.common.sanity.SanityManager.ASSERT(SanityManager.java:98)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.cache.CacheEntry.unkeepForRemove(CacheEntry.java:217)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.services.cache.ConcurrentCache.remove(ConcurrentCache.java:446)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.conn.GenericLanguageConnectionContext.removeStatement(GenericLanguageConnectionContext.java:898)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.GenericStatement.prepMinion(GenericStatement.java:516)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.GenericStatement.prepare(GenericStatement.java:88)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.conn.GenericLanguageConnectionContext.prepareInternalStatement(GenericLanguageConnectionContext.java:794)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.jdbc.EmbedStatement.execute(EmbedStatement.java:606)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.jdbc.EmbedStatement.execute(EmbedStatement.java:555)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.ij.executeImmediate(ij.java:329)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.utilMain.doCatch(utilMain.java:508)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.utilMain.runScriptGuts(utilMain.java:350)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.utilMain.go(utilMain.java:248)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.mtTestCase.runMe(mtTestCase.java:246)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.tools.ij.mtTester.run(mtTester.java:91)<br> at java.lang.Thread.run(Thread.java:619)<br> Cleanup action completed</p> 
                  <hr> 
                  <p>In stress.log:</p> 
                  <hr> 
                  <p>Tester8: insert2 Fri Jul 18 04:15:12 CEST 2008<br> Tester6: TERMINATING due to unexpected error:<br> FatalException: XJ001: Java exception: 'ASSERT FAILED: org.apache.derby.shared.common.sanity.AssertFailure'.<br> Tester1: SELECT2 Fri Jul 18 04:15:13 CEST 2008<br> Tester8: stopping on request after 820 iterations<br> Tester10: stopping on request after 859 iterations<br> Tester1: stopping on request after 847 iterations<br> Tester7: TERMINATING due to unexpected error:<br> FatalException: XJ001: Java exception: 'ASSERT FAILED: org.apache.derby.shared.common.sanity.AssertFailure'.<br> Tester9: stopping on request after 722 iterations<br> Tester3: stopping on request after 880 iterations<br> Tester5: stopping on request after 858 iterations<br> Tester4: stopping on request after 839 iterations<br> WARNING: testers didn't die willingly, so I'm going to kill 'em.<br> This may result in connection resources that aren't cleaned up<br> (e.g. you may see problems in the final script run with deadlocks).</p> 
                  <hr> 
                  <p>A few runs on a similar but slightly slower machine didn't experience the same failure, so the bug is likely timing dependent.<br> I'll perform some more runs and see how hard it is to reproduce.</p> 
                  <p>I haven't investigated what will happen in an insane build.</p>
