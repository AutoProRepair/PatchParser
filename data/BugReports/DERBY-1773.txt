               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>When the select query used in an updatable result set has column aliases, a syntax error is thrown when executing ResultSet.insertRow() and ResultSet.updateRow(). The problem is seen on embedded and client. Repro is attached.</p> 
                  <p>Exception in thread "main" ERROR 42X14: 'A1' is not a column in table or VTI 'APP.T'.<br> at org.apache.derby.iapi.error.StandardException.newException(StandardException.java:316)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.compile.ResultColumn.bindResultColumnByName(ResultColumn.java:677)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.compile.ResultColumnList.bindResultColumnsByName(ResultColumnList.java:682)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.compile.ResultSetNode.bindResultColumns(ResultSetNode.java:683)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.compile.SelectNode.bindResultColumns(SelectNode.java:742)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.compile.UpdateNode.bind(UpdateNode.java:349)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.GenericStatement.prepMinion(GenericStatement.java:345)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.GenericStatement.prepare(GenericStatement.java:111)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.sql.conn.GenericLanguageConnectionContext.prepareInternalStatement(GenericLanguageConnectionContext.java:723)<br> at org.apache.derby.impl.jdbc.EmbedResultSet.updateRow(EmbedResultSet.java:3734)<br> at Alias.main(Alias.java:15)</p>
