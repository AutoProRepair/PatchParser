               <div class="mod-content">
                <div id="description-val" class="field-ignore-highlight"> 
                 <div class="user-content-block"> 
                  <p>FileUtils.doCopyDirectory(..) and .FileUtils.doCopyFile(..) both call the setLastModified() method, but fail to check if it succeeded or not.</p> 
                  <p>Surely if the caller has asked for the date to be preserved, failure to do so should be reported somehow?</p>
